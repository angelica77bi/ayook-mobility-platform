# 🚗 Ayook — Mobility Platform in Bali

Ayook is a scooter rental platform designed to simplify how people move around Bali.

It connects travelers with local rental providers through a clean, transparent, and easy-to-use booking experience.

---

## 🌴 Overview

Getting around Bali can be challenging:

- Traffic is heavy  
- Transportation options are fragmented  
- Traditional scooter rentals are often unclear or inconsistent  

Ayook aims to make mobility simple, reliable, and accessible.

---

## ✨ Features

- Online scooter booking in minutes  
- Transparent pricing with flexible durations  
- Free essentials (helmet, charger, phone holder)  
- Delivery & pickup service across Bali  
- Optional insurance and support
